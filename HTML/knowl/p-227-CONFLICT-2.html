Let \(a\in F\) and suppose \(b\) and \(c\) are such that \(a+c = 0_F\) and \(a+b = 0_F\text{.}\) Then \(a+c = a+b\text{,}\) and we may add \(b\) (or \(c\)) to both sides to obtain \(b+(a+b) = (b+a)+b = 0_F + b = b\) and \(c+(a+c) = (c+a)+c = 0_F + c = c\text{.}\) Thus \(b=c\text{.}\)
